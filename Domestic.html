National Tour Packages by Destinations


Package tours are excursions or holidays which “package” a variety of services together to make a single “combined” trip. Commonly they combine such things as transport, accommodation and meals. They may also include the provision of a tour guide and/or leader. Tours can be long or short in duration and distance. They may be a one-day or overnight package, or they could be a period of a month or more. The global religious travel’s market is growing tremendously as travelling is now becoming easy and cheap. However, there has been scant literature studying traveller’s behaviour toward religious tourism, despite the fact that this sector is growing as the numbers of Muslim travellers are increasing each year.
